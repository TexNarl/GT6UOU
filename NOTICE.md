GT6UOU is an experimental modern NeoForge port/reimplementation inspired by GregTech 6.

This repository does not include the reference repositories listed below. They were used for research, comparison, and compatibility study while designing this project.

The coordinate hierarchy, irregular cellular-region ownership model, ordered
region-task architecture, continent-field semantics, regional river graphs,
midpoint-fractal river paths, partition indexing, and weighted shore/river
terrain modifiers, as well as the separated height/density/surface generation
pipeline in
`gregtech.worldgen.earth` are a modern GT6UOU implementation based on
the EUPL-1.2 world-generation design of TerraFirmaCraft and the LGPL-3.0
adaptations in TerraFirmaGreg Core. Source files carrying an explicit derived
design notice retain those attributions.
